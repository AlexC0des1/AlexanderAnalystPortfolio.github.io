Microsoft Excel Projects

Analyzing Financial data

In this project I took the income sheets, the balance sheets, and cash flow statements from BMW and Volkswagen over a 4 year time period and executed ratio, vertical, and horizontal analysis on each sheet. This provided insights into these companies' performance over time and allowed me to see what I can watch out for in the future while looking at these statements. I then created a small interactive dashboard in Excel which, while very basic, allowed me to show the important ratios that needed to be addressed in a comprehensive comparative report.
